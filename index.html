Our Core Focus

We are registered under the Govt. of West Bengal Act XXVI of 1961. Our mission is built on facts and urgent needs.

Underprivileged Children

More than 10 crore Children in India are uneducated. Girl children are often kept out of school or ignored.

Our Focus: To make them aware of their rights and give them basic education in our balwadi. We aim to break the cycle of poverty through literacy.

Women Empowerment

More than 60% of Women don't have access to basic education in India, which is a big setback for society.

Our Focus: To make them self-sufficient by giving them vocational training in Tailoring and Nursing, enabling them to earn a dignified livelihood.

Physically Challenged

These special children are the most neglected part of society. They have dreams but face physical or mental constraints.

Our Aim: To fulfill their dreams unconditionally and provide them with the support they need to smile.

Support The Aged

There are thousands of aged people who have forgotten to smile since ages, often abandoned in their last stages of life.

Our Aim: To provide them basic food, shelter, and medical aid, restoring their dignity.
